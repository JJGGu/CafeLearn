Mikaël Portannier, representing France is the 2025 World Coffee Roasting Champion!

RankingsAnnouncements
Apr 27
Written By Specialty Coffee Association

Congratulations to Mikaël Portannier, representing France, the 2025 World Coffee Roasting Champion! The World Coffee Roasting Championship has been taking place at the Specialty Coffee Expo in Houston, Texas, United States for the past four days, where 23 competitors have been representing their Competition Bodies in a dazzling display of technical mastery.

2025 World Coffee Roasting Championship Final Ranking:

  1. Mikaël Portannier, France

  2. Ashton Huang, Taiwan

  3. Naruepon Wuttipappinyo, Thailand

  4. 宋春丹 Song Chundan, China

See the Final Rankings
