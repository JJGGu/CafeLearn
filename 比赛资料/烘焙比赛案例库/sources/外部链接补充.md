## 2023 Taufan Mokoginta 技术参数访谈

- 标题：`Taufan Mokoginta World Coffee Roasting Champion 2023`
- 链接：[Creators Coffee](https://www.creators.coffee/blogs/creators-magazine/taufan-mokoginta-world-coffee-roasting-champion-2023)
- 价值：
  - 有非常少见的具体参数
  - 提到 `Stronghold S7 Pro / S7X`
  - 提到 `gas / air / turning point / yellow / first crack / drop / DTR / color`

## 2023 Andrew Coe 季军技术访谈

- 标题：`Interview with Andrew Coe, 3rd Place World Coffee Roasting Champion`
- 链接：[Coffee People Zine](https://coffeepeoplezine.com/2023/11/09/interview-with-andrew-coe-3rd-place-world-coffee-roasting-champion/)
- 价值：
  - 直接写出 `Joper 6kg` 的火力与风门分段
  - 写出单品和拼配两套比赛思路
  - 有“先杯测，再选曲线”的典型比赛逻辑

## 2025 Mikael Portannier 访谈

- 标题：`Mikael Portannier: Championing Coffee Roasting with the ROEST Sample Roasters`
- 链接：[ROEST](https://roestcoffee.com/stories/mikael-portannier-profile/)
