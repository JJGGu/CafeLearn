Bullet Points: US Roasting Champ Andrew Coe

Nov 30, 2023

US Coffee Roasting Champion 2023, Andrew Coe, visited the Aillio office in Taiwan during the World Coffee Championships. Andrew has used the Bullet since 2018 and in this interview, he shares his story, his path to becoming the Champion and his best advice for... all of us Bullet users!

(Shortly after this interview, Andrew Coe achieved another milestone by landing 3rd place in the 2023 World Coffee Roasting Championship!)
